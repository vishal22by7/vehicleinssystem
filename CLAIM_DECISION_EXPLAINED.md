   - **Example**: "Photo shows a bicycle, not your insured vehicle"

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Q1: If my claim is rejected, does it mean my car is completely destroyed?
**A:** No! Rejection means insurance won't pay, not that your car is destroyed. Your car might still be repairable, but you'd pay for repairs yourself.

### Q2: Can I appeal a rejected claim?
**A:** Yes! You can contact the Claims Review Committee to appeal. All rejections go through committee review (IRDAI requirement).

### Q3: Why do I get less money than repair cost?
**A:** Insurance payout is based on your policy premium and damage severity, not actual repair cost. This is standard practice.

### Q4: What if AI makes a mistake?
**A:** That's why we have manual review! If AI confidence is low or damage is moderate, a human expert reviews it.

### Q5: How long does it take?
**A:** 
- Auto-Approved: Hours
- Manual Review: 1-3 days
- Rejected: Usually within 24 hours
